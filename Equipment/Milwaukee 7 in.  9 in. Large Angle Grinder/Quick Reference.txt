Milwaukee 7 in.  9 in. Large Angle Grinder

Summary

A heavy-duty, cordless angle grinder built for demanding metal cutting and grinding operations. Designed for large-diameter wheels (7″ or 9″), it delivers high speed and torque, ideal for extrication and demolition tasks in fire/rescue environments.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Wheel Size: 7″/9″
    Speed: Up to 8,500 RPM
    Motor: Brushless M18 FUEL
    Operation: Use dual-hand grip and safety guard
    Maintenance: Inspect wheels and keep vents clear
