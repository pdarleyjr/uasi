Technical & Operational Details

    Impact Energy: Approximately 2 Joules per blow.
    Speed: Up to 1,400 RPM with 4,900 BPM (blows per minute).
    Modes: Drill-only, hammer-drill, and hammer-only (chipping) modes.
    SDS System: Uses SDS-plus bits for fast and secure bit changes.
    Integration: Shares battery compatibility with the entire M18 tool family.
