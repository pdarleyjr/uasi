9″ Cordless Cut Off Saw

Summary

This cordless cut-off saw kit delivers high power and portability, ideal for cutting concrete, metal, and impact glass. Powered by DeWalt’s 60V MAX FLEXVOLT system, it combines a brushless motor with a 9″ blade and comes with a 9Ah battery for extended run-time.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Blade Diameter: 9″
    Speed: Up to 6,500 RPM
    Cutting Depth: ~3–3/4″
    Dust Suppression: Integrated water ports
    Battery: 9Ah, part of FLEXVOLT system
    Safety: Always use dual-hand operation and proper PPE
