Milwaukee 4.5″5″ Angle Grinder

Summary

A versatile, cordless angle grinder that offers the portability of an 18V tool combined with the cutting/grinding power of a 4.5″/5″ grinder. It features a paddle switch for improved safety (continuous hold required) and is designed for light to medium duty work.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Wheel Compatibility: 4.5″/5″
    Speed: Up to ~9,000 RPM
    Switch: Paddle (no lock-on)
    Battery: 18V platform
    Safety: Use proper PPE and never bypass safety switch
