Paratech VSK V2 CONTROLLER

Summary

The VSK V2 Controller is an electronic control unit for regulating the pressure and inflation of Paratech air bag systems. It allows for precise control over multiple bag circuits in rig rescue operations.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Channels: Dual-channel pressure control
    Display: Digital pressure readout
    Integration: For Paratech air bag systems only
    Maintenance: Regular calibration recommended
