Milwaukee Deep Cut Band Saw

Summary

A cordless deep cut band saw designed for aggressive metal and concrete cutting. The tool features a high-torque motor and deep cutting capability for precision cutting in extrication, demolition, or structural modification scenarios.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Cutting Method: Band blade deep cut system
    Battery: M18 system (interchangeable)
    Motor: High-torque brushless
    Operation: Secure workpiece and use guide rails
    Safety: Use full PPE and verify blade tension before use
