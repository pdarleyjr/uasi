Paratech SUPPORTER X2

Summary

The Supporter X2 is a versatile accessory designed for anchoring and stabilizing loads during rescue or extrication operations. Its integrated anchor ring provides secure connection points for additional restraints or lifting devices.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Anchor: High-strength steel anchor ring
    Integration: Works with Paratech stabilization systems
    Installation: Mount on a structurally sound surface
    Maintenance: Inspect for rust/damage regularly
