Technical & Operational Details

    Stabilization Capacity: Typically rated around 16 kN (~3,600 lbs of support force).
    Design: Telescoping aluminum construction with auto-locking pins.
    Integrated Components: V-shaped head, base plate with non-slip grip, and integrated ratchet strap.
    Adjustability: Quick extension and fine adjustment (in increments of a few millimeters) for precise stabilization.
    Integration: Often used with other extrication tools (e.g., cribbing, spreaders) to secure vehicles before forcible entry.
