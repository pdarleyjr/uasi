Safety & Best Practices

    PPE: Use gloves and safety footwear during setup.
    Deployment: Always position the base on solid ground; maintain a safe angle (approximately 45–60°).
    Operation: Verify that the V-snout engages a solid metal portion of the vehicle.
    Maintenance: Check for dents or worn serrations on the V-head; clean and lubricate the telescoping mechanism periodically.
