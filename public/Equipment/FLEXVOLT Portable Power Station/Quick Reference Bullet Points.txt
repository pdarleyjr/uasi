FLEXVOLT Portable Power Station (Tool Only) – S/N# DCB1800B (DEWALT)
Summary

The FLEXVOLT Portable Power Station is a battery-based, high-output power source designed to drive DeWalt’s cordless 60V MAX and 20V MAX tools. It is engineered for portability, rapid charging, and reliable operation in demanding fire/rescue scenarios.

Quick Reference Bullet Points

    Peak Power: ~1800W output
    Compatibility: Works with 60V/20V tools
    Connections: AC, DC, USB outputs available
    Maintenance: Keep contacts clean, store in dry environment
    Safety: Use proper PPE and follow lockout procedures
